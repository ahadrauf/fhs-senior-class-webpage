Announcements

The Class of 2016 Grad Night will be held at Disneyland from Friday, 6/10, to Sunday, 6/12.

To receive the latest updates on details for Grad Night, sign up using this form. Parents are welcome to sign up as well.


To see pictures from last year's Grad Nite trip, check out the Pictures page.


To register for the trip, fill out Form #6 on the Forms page and turn it in to the front office at lunch. Although the forms are due by March 20, 2016, bus seating is first-come, first-serve, so register quickly. If you need to have medications , either prescription or over the counter, with you on the trip, you must also fill out Form #7. If you have food allergies, you must fill out Form #8.

The forms you'll have to turn into the front office are:

  • Registration Form (Form #6)
  • Permission Slip (Form #6)
  • Parents Approval Form and Student Waiver (if student is under 18) (Form #6)
  • Participant's Waiver (if student is 18 or older) (Form #6)
  • Medication Consent Form (if you must have medication with you on the trip) (Form #7)
  • Food Allergy Plan Form (if you have a food allergy) (Form #8)

To begin looking at the parks, view the California Adventure Park map here or the Disneyland map here.

Important Dates

December 1, 2015: First Deposit due

You must turn in your first deposit, a minimum of $50, to the front office at lunch by this date to guarantee a spot on the trip. You should turn in the deposit in a box labeled "Grad Nite" in the Student Services section.

May 20, 2016: Grad Night Forms Due

Turn in your Disneyland Grad Night forms and bus assignments in the front office at lunch by this date. You can find all the necessary forms in Form #6 on the Forms page.

June 10-12: Disneyland Grad Night Trip

See below for the trip schedule. More info will be available soon!

Schedule

Friday, June 10
  • 11:00 pm — Check-in starts in Big Gym on Foothill campus (must bring Foothill Student ID)
  • 12:00 am — Departure
Saturday, June 11
  • 7:00 am — Arrival at Disneyland; eat breakfast at Bubba Gump Shrimp Company Restaurant
  • 9:00 am — Arrival at Disneyland Resort; students are left to explore the park on their own
  • 6:00 pm — Check-in; afterwards, students continue to explore the park on their own
  • 10:00 pm — Check-in; afterwards, students go to a private Grad Nite party at Disney California Adventures Park
Sunday, June 12
  • 2:00 am — Party ends
  • 2:15 am — Departure from Disney California Adventure Park
  • 10:00 am — Students arrive back at Foothill campus; students are responsible for themselves after they arrive
